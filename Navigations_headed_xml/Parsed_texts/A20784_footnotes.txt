Compiler's dedication signed: Francis Drake.
Printer's name from STC.
The portrait comes in two versions: (1) signed by Robert Vaughan, with English verses beginning "The worlde suruaied .."; (2) an anonymous copy in reverse, with Latin verse beginning "Drake perorati novit ..".
The role of Sir Francis Drake (the nephew) as compiler has been disputed: see Wagner, Henry R.  Sir Francis Drake's voyage around the world. San Francisco, 1926, p. 286.
Reproduction of the original in the Henry E. Huntington Library and Art Gallery.
